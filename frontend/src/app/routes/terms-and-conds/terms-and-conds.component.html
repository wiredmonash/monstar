2. Content Guidelines

  • Adhere to the Monash University Student Charter
    (specifically Principles 1.1 and 1.2 regarding respect and academic integrity).
  • Avoid abusive/offensive language.
    Reviews containing profanity, discrimination, harassment, or bullying will be removed.
  • Post genuine experiences.
    Do not submit fake reviews, spam, or content intended to manipulate ratings.
  • Stay on-topic.
    Reviews must focus on your experiences in the unit, not unrelated topics (e.g. politics, solicitations).

3. Prohibited Content

We reserve the right to remove content that:

  • Describes of links to illegal activities (e.g., academic misconduct, copyright infringement).
  • Contains malicious code, threats of harm, or advocates violence.
  • Involves impersonation or false representation.
  • Reflects a conflict of interest (e.g., staff reviewing their own unit).

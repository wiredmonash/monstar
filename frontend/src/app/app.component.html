FAQs

MonSTAR is an online platform designed for Monash University’s Faculty of IT students to browse, review, and share feedback on academic units, helping others make informed decisions.

You can submit a review by clicking the "Write a Review" option in the menu. Simply enter the unit details and your feedback. Make sure your review follows the community guidelines.
